Thu 9 Oct 2025 — Takayama (free day)

Overview
Second night in Takayama. Explore historic Sanmachi-suji, morning markets and local sake breweries.

Travel

No intercity travel today.

Description

Edo-period merchant town in the Hida mountains; famed for master carpenters and one of Japan’s most beautiful festivals.

Read full description
Takayama flourished as a castle and merchant town in the Edo period and is renowned for its master carpenters, the ‘Hida no Takumi’. Its preserved old streets (Sanmachi‑suji) hold dark‑timbered machiya, sake breweries marked by cedar balls, and craft shops. The Takayama Festival, held in spring and autumn, is famed for ornate floats (yatai) and mechanical dolls. Local flavours include Hida beef, pickles and mountain vegetables.
Read more: Takayama — Wikipedia
